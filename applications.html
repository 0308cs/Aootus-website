Use case overview

Application-based product selection

Buyers often start from project conditions. This page helps connect use cases with recommended system types and accessory combinations.

Selection inputs

Site type, curtain weight, opening width, mounting surface, and maintenance expectations.

Hotel

Recommended: aluminum track system, blackout curtain carriers, matched brackets.

Hospital

Recommended: ceiling-mounted tracks, flexible tracks, compatible curtain hooks.

Office

Recommended: slim track systems or simple rods for meeting rooms and glass partitions.

Apartment

Recommended: standard track packages or rod sets with repeatable finish and packaging.

Large Window

Recommended: reinforced rod systems, stronger brackets, and wider support spacing.

Room Divider

Recommended: flexible track or motor-compatible track depending on opening scale.
