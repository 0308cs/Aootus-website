Custom project support

OEM and private label supply

We support practical customization for buyers who need consistent product supply under their own brand or project requirement.

Can be customized

Length, finish, accessory set, labels, cartons, insert cards, and packaging structure.

Process

Simple OEM workflow

1. Requirement Review

Confirm product category, market, drawings, target quantity, and packaging needs.

2. Sample and Finish

Check profile, accessories, surface finish, and label layout before bulk order.

3. Bulk Production

Arrange production schedule, accessory matching, and carton plan for shipment.

4. QC and Shipment

Inspect finished goods, confirm packing, and prepare export delivery documents.
